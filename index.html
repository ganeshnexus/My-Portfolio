About Me

Leadership built on execution, trust, and scale

I am an Operations Manager with 14+ years of experience in the US IT staffing industry, leading end-to-end operations across recruitment, bench sales, delivery, compliance, and client management.

I started my career hands-on in bench sales and grew into leadership roles by building teams, fixing broken processes, and delivering consistent results. That journey shaped my leadership style. I stay close to execution, set clear expectations, and focus on accountability without micromanagement.
